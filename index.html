Our Mission

San Joaquin Valley Town Hall is a nonprofit organization that is run by an all-volunteer board of directors. Our mission
is to bring nationally and internationally renowned, thought-provoking speakers who inform, educate and entertain our
audience! As one of our members told us:

"each year I give a ticket package to each of my family members. I think of it as the gift of knowledge...
and that is priceless."
